What is Managed Services?

Every business or organization struggles every day to counter the ever-evolving cyber threats. Maintaining an incident management team internally costs a fair amount of budget as it requires constant vigilance. This is where CyberAlertz can help you. Our team helps you manage cybersecurity needs realistically and cost-effectively based on the size and scope of your business.

Benefits

Time Efficient

More time to focus on other areas of your business as the staff does not have to continuously resolve incidents.

Improve Security

Reliable protection with guaranteed service level agreements for clients. All potential risks are shifted from your organization to our team.

Access to Unique Tools

Provide extra knowledge on cybersecurity expertise your in-house team may not know about.

Our Services

Down below is a brief introduction of our services if you have any questions do not hesitate to ask us on our Contact Us page

Firewall

Our team provides firewall services such as firewall auditing, configuration and monitoring. Furthermore, we have co-managing firewall services which allow your team to work alongside with us instead of us taking over every aspect of the firewall.

Intrusion Detection System(IDS)

Our IDS detects an attack that is being carried out by unauthorized personnel by constantly monitoring the
changes in servers and files. Once the IDS has detected unauthorized file alterations, it will generate an alarm notifying that security has been breached. Some of the different IDS we use are signature-based, anomaly-based or host intrusion detection systems.

Data Loss Prevention(DLP)

DLP allows us to discover and locate information in databases that needs to be protected. By monitoring
usage of data, we will be able to enforce encryption policies and prevent transmission of sensitive data to outsiders. CyberAlertz gives you complete visibility of your system so you fully understand what is happening all the time.

Disaster Recovery

Our team provides continuous replication of data and information to enable rapid recovery in the event of cyber attacks or IT outages. Our disaster recovery system is highly customizable in design and architecture so you can get back into business quickly.

Identity and Access Management

By limiting and tracking employee access, we can ensure hackers will not gain access to your systems. CyberAlertz provides a few types of user authentication such as Single Sign-On, Multifactor Authentication and Privileged Access Management. We make sure to only allow specific users to access data to prevent unauthorized use.
